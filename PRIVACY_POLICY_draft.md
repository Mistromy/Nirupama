# Nirupama Privacy Policy / EULA
## Data Privacy:
### Processed Data:
- voice messages
- messages mentioning/replying to the bot
- message frequency

### Stored Data:
- Data collected by the pinging of the bot will be temporarily stored for use by the ai model
- Only the last 10 messages are stored
- These messages can only be accessed by the ai

### Data Sharing:
- Data sent to the ai will be sent to and processed by Groq AI.

### Data Processing:
- Your voice messages will be processed, and have the content extracted as text.
